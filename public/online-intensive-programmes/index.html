8-WEEK PROGRAMME

Russian Law for International Lawyers

A practical introduction to the foundations of Russian law for international legal professionals, researchers, and institutions. The programme is delivered in English with references to original Russian legal sources where appropriate.

  • Sources and structure of Russian law
  • Constitutional framework and institutions
  • Civil, commercial, contract, and dispute-resolution topics
  • Human rights, comparative perspectives, and legal terminology

Format: weekly live seminars, reading materials, certificate of completion, and discussion group.

12-WEEK PROGRAMME

Russian for Lawyers

An intensive legal Russian programme for lawyers, academics, diplomats, compliance specialists, and students who need to understand and use Russian legal language in professional contexts.

  • Legal vocabulary in legislation, contracts, and correspondence
  • Court terminology and judicial procedure
  • Contract language and commercial legal documents
  • Drafting exercises and case-based learning

Format: live online seminars, practical exercises, authentic legal materials, and certificate of completion.
